The 15-Minute Room Reset

One room. One timer. Five steps — always in the same order, so you never decide what to do next:

  • TRASH — grab a bag, collect only garbage. Everything else doesn't exist yet.
  • DISHES & LAUNDRY — gather into two piles. Don't wash. Don't fold. Just gather.
  • DOESN'T-LIVE-HERE — basket everything that belongs elsewhere. Deliver later, never mid-reset.
  • HOMES — what's left goes to its home (or the room's "landing bin" — allowed!).
  • SURFACE — one wipe of the anchor surface. Timer rings. You're done. Really done.

Sorting by category instead of item-by-item means up to 10× fewer decisions — which is exactly what an ADHD brain needs to keep moving.
